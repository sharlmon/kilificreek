Industry

Where Knowledge Meets Creativity

The KCF Industry programme pairs daytime workshops and masterclasses with evening outdoor screenings, creating a unique space where professional development and artistic celebration go hand in hand.

During the day, filmmakers, artists, and industry professionals come together for hands-on workshops, in-depth masterclasses, moderated Q&A sessions, and professional exchanges. These sessions cover everything from storytelling and cinematography to distribution, funding, and the business of film in Africa.

As the sun sets over the creek, the programme transitions to open-air screenings — transforming the Kilifi waterfront into an immersive cinematic experience that brings communities, filmmakers, and audiences together.
