About Us

A Festival Rooted in Place

Kilifi Creek Festival is more than an event — it’s a movement to decentralize the arts, bringing creativity beyond city centers and into natural, community-rich environments. It champions the idea that artistic excellence can emerge from mangroves, ocean rhythms, and local stories as much as from urban capitals.
